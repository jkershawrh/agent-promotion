= Lab Guide
:toc: left
:toclevels: 3

This is a self-paced lab for attendees who want to experience how autonomous agents earn, lose, and are refused authority.
You will watch an agent be blocked, earn a promotion, go through a human-ratified promotion, and get demoted when it fails.

== Prerequisites

* A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari)
* The Agent Promotion Line demo URL provided by your lab instructor
* No local software installation required -- everything runs in the browser

== Step 1: Open the Demo

When you open the Agent Promotion Line URL, you will see a dark-themed dashboard with a sidebar on the left.

. The sidebar shows five layers: The Hook, The Earn, The Depth, The Floor, The Close
. Each layer is numbered 0 through 4
. You can jump to any layer by clicking it or pressing the corresponding number key
. The Reset button (or press `R`) clears all state

== Step 2: Watch the Hook (Layer 0)

Click *The Hook* in the sidebar or press `0`.

You will see a pipeline of five authority tiers (T0 through T4) and a queue of pending actions.

. Click *Send action to the gate*
. Watch for:
** The gate decision card appears with a red *REFUSED* animation
** The pipeline edge from T0 to T1 shows *BLOCKED*
** The agent proposed an action with consequence score 30, but its ceiling is 0

[cols="1,3"]
|===
| What happened | What it means

| *Gate refused*
| The agent at T0 (PROBATION) cannot execute any action with consequence above 0. The gate enforces this. The agent is advisory only.
|===

The key idea: restraint is guaranteed, not hoped for.

== Step 3: Watch the Earn (Layer 1)

Click *The Earn* in the sidebar or press `1`.

. Click *Build track record (55 verified chains)* -- watch the chain dots fill in green
. Click *Evaluate for promotion* -- watch the agent move from T0 to T1 in the pipeline
. Click *Test a low-risk action at T1* -- watch the gate return *ALLOWED*

[cols="1,3"]
|===
| What happened | What it means

| *Track record filled*
| 55 verified chains: each one a predict, act, verify cycle recorded in the ledger.

| *Promotion T0 to T1*
| The agent met the criteria: 50+ chains and 60%+ rolling accuracy. Its ceiling rose from 0 to 25.

| *Low-risk action allowed*
| Consequence 10 is within the T1 ceiling of 25. The gate allows it.
|===

The key idea: promotion is earned by evidence, not granted by configuration.

== Step 4: Try HITL Mode (Layer 2)

Click *The Depth* in the sidebar or press `2`.

The agent is at T2 (TRUSTED, ceiling 50) in HITL_HIGH_TIER mode.

. Click *Evaluate for T2 to T3 promotion* -- the system does not auto-promote
. Read the ratification case: chain count, rolling accuracy, notable successes, near misses
. Click *Approve* to ratify the promotion (or *Reject* to deny it)

[cols="1,3"]
|===
| What happened | What it means

| *Ratification queued*
| The promotion into T3 (AUTONOMOUS, ceiling 100) is high-consequence. The system assembled a case and queued it for a human.

| *Human approved*
| Only after the human ratified did the promotion apply. The system will not self-grant high authority.
|===

The key idea: the agent cannot grade its own promotion.

NOTE: In FULL_AUTO mode, this same promotion would apply automatically. HITL_HIGH_TIER gates only the high tiers.

== Step 5: Trigger a Demotion (Layer 3)

Click *The Floor* in the sidebar or press `3`.

The agent is at T3 (AUTONOMOUS, ceiling 100). It took an action at its ceiling that was later falsified.

. Click *Falsified outcome arrives*
. Watch for:
** The demotion card drops into view: T3 to T2
** The failed chain correlation ID appears in red
** The ledger trail shows the demotion entry immediately

[cols="1,3"]
|===
| What happened | What it means

| *Immediate demotion*
| The agent was demoted the moment the falsified outcome was verified. No meeting. No delay. No human gate.

| *Ledger entry recorded*
| The demotion entry is in the ARE Immutable Ledger with the falsified correlation ID. The full chain is auditable.
|===

The key idea: demotion never waits on a human. Pulling authority is immediate.

== Step 6: Explore the Roster (The Close)

Click *The Close* in the sidebar or press `4`.

. Review the roster: all registered agents with their current tier, ceiling, and mode
. Read the belief sentence: "I can trust it the way I would trust a good employee, except it cannot exceed its authority, cannot hide a mistake, and cannot have a bad day."
. Review the Red Hat platform story

== Closing

You have now:

. Seen an autonomous agent blocked from acting above its authority (the gate)
. Watched it earn a promotion through a verified track record (the earn)
. Seen a high-authority promotion require human ratification (HITL)
. Watched trust be walked back immediately when an action was falsified (demotion)
. Reviewed the full audit trail in the ARE Immutable Ledger (provenance)

The key takeaway: *agent authority is earned by evidence, enforced by the gate, and revoked without delay. Governed AI on the platform you already run.*
