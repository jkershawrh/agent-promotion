= Agent Promotion Line
:page-layout: home

== What is the Agent Promotion Line?

A governance layer that grants, raises, and revokes each agent's authority to act, based on its verified track record. Authority is the dynamic form of consequence-scoping: an agent's consequence ceiling moves with its proven record.

[quote]
____
I can trust it the way I would trust a good employee, except it cannot exceed its authority, cannot hide a mistake, and cannot have a bad day.
____

== The Five Tiers

[cols="1,2,2,2"]
|===
| Tier | Name | Consequence Ceiling | Can Execute

| *T0*
| PROBATION
| Advisory only (0)
| Propose only. Every action routes to a human.

| *T1*
| VERIFIED
| Low (25)
| Autonomous on low-blast-radius actions.

| *T2*
| TRUSTED
| Medium (50)
| Autonomous on medium-blast-radius actions.

| *T3*
| AUTONOMOUS
| High (100)
| Autonomous on high-blast-radius actions.

| *T4*
| PRINCIPAL
| Critical (1000)
| Autonomous on critical actions. Entry always requires human ratification.
|===

== Three Visible Behaviors

. *Earn.* An agent with a clean verified record is promoted and its consequence ceiling rises.
. *Revoke.* An agent whose action is later falsified is demoted immediately and its ceiling drops.
. *Refuse.* An agent attempting an action above its current ceiling is blocked, never silently allowed.

== Two Operating Modes

- *FULL_AUTO:* promotions and demotions apply automatically when criteria are met or violated.
- *HITL_HIGH_TIER:* low-tier promotions apply automatically; promotions into high-consequence tiers require a human to ratify before they take effect.

Demotion is always immediate and automatic in both modes. Only promotion into high authority is ever gated by a human.

== Red Hat + Intel Positioning

- *OpenShift* is the platform where agents and the promotion service run
- *ARE Immutable Ledger* provides the tamper-evident compliance trail
- *Apache 2.0* -- the promotion pipeline, the gate, the ledger client are all inspectable
- *CPU-native* -- governance runs on the infrastructure you already have, no GPU required
- *Compliance-ready* -- EU AI Act Article 12 traceability, SOC 2 Type II, NIST AI RMF
